Sara Goodie // Cybersecurity Analyst

Entry-level cybersecurity professional focused on security operations, threat detection, and log analysis — with hands-on experience building and testing labs and supporting IT operations during my internship with the Detroit Pistons.

I tend to think through multiple scenarios and anticipate what could happen next when approaching a problem. Combined with a creative, outside-the-box mindset, this helps me recognize patterns and behaviors that aren't immediately obvious — which is what drew me to cybersecurity and threat detection.

Through my labs, I've simulated real-world attacks such as brute-force attempts, credential dumping, and lateral movement, then analyzed system and network logs to understand how those actions appear and can be detected. I've worked with SIEM and endpoint monitoring tools (including Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, and Sysmon) to practice log correlation, basic alerting, and threat analysis.

During my internship, I gained experience in an enterprise environment — Active Directory, endpoint security tools, and troubleshooting across users and systems. I also had the opportunity to attend live game operations and shadow IT staff, giving me insight into how technology and support systems function in real-time, high-demand environments.

I'm continuing to develop my ability to detect, analyze, and respond to security events in real-world environments, with a focus on practical, hands-on learning.
